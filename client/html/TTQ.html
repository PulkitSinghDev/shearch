To the Queen

  1. As the dial hand tells o’er
  2. The same hours it had before,
  3. Circular account still lending,
  4. So, most mighty Queen we pray,
  5. Like the dial day by day
  6. You may lead the seasons on,
  7. Making new when old are gone,
  8. That the babe which now is young
  9. And hath yet no use of tongue
  10. Many a Shrovetide here may bow
  11. To that empress I do now,
  12. That these children of these lords,
